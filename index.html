A Love Letter for Mokka

My dearest Mokka,

Across Egypt to Tunisia, the maps say we’re far, but every day you’re the closest part of my world. I love the way we find each other in little routines: your sleepy nights where you forget half of what you say the next day, our late calls when the sky goes deep blue, the jokes that make us laugh until we forget the distance. You turn ordinary moments into something I replay like my favorite song.

My love, I want you to know that nothing, absolutely nothing, will ever diminish what I feel for you. Not distance, not silences, not hard times. On the contrary, every obstacle reminds me of how precious you are to me. I still dream every day of our future together. I am obsessed with it and can’t wait until we achieve it. It is the only thing I think about. From living in Dubai, to exploring New York, to strolling in Japan, to buying you a Porsche, to having our dream family—where I come home from work tired after a bad day and I see you and then everything feels perfect.

I gave you my word before and I’ll give it again. I will make you feel comfortable. I will make you love me again. I will make you want me again—and most importantly, I’ll be the first one you ever trust in your life. You are my safe place, my adventure, my best friend. I adore you.

Until I can hold you, keep one hand on this page and one hand on your heart—mine’s right there with you.

Always yours,
— Your “good” boy
